
There once lived in a town of Persia two brothers, one named <name>Cassim</name> and the other <name>Ali Baba</name>. Their father divided a small inheritance equally between them. <name>Cassim</name> married a very rich wife, and became a wealthy merchant. <name>Ali Baba</name> married a woman as poor as himself, and lived by cutting wood, and bringing it upon three asses into the town to sell.
